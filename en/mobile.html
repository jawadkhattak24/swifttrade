Mobile ST5

GO MARKETS provides global investors with the GO MARKETS mobile platform, so that you are no longer restricted by desktop computers, but can keep track of market dynamics through smartphones or tablets. The ST5 mobile platform supports Apple and Android systems. You only need to log in with your ST5 account information to view real-time quotes, execute transactions (including opening, closing, setting, and modifying stop-profit/stop-loss orders, etc.), and get the market News and other information. Allows you to easily connect and conduct transactions anytime, anywhere, and improve your account activity.

The main functions of the ST5 mobile platform:
Quickly place an order and view the position on the chart
Support a variety of order transaction types
View account information, profit and loss reports, etc.
30 technical indicators, real-time interactive charts
Push the latest market news in time
Keep all data in the account synchronized
Support IOS and Android system

Log in to the ST5 mobile platform
Enter "GO MARKETS" in the search bar of the mobile store and choose to install. You can also download and install directly.
After running the application, if you already have a real or simulated account with GO MARKETS, please click the "Log in to an existing account" option.
